About Us

A Different Kind of Safari Company

Ghikas Adventures is a Tanzania-based safari and boutique tourism company founded on a simple but uncompromising belief: that true travel is not just about what you see — it is about what you feel, learn, and carry home with you.

Headquartered in Morogoro, we sit at the heart of one of Africa's most biodiverse and underexplored regions. We are not a mass-market operator. We are storytellers, naturalists, and guides who happen to run a tour company.

My Story

Jonathan Gregory Ghikas

I was born and raised in Morogoro, Tanzania — a town that sits on the edge of some of the most extraordinary wilderness on the African continent. Growing up here, nature was never a destination. It was simply home.

My formal journey into wildlife began at the University of Dar es Salaam, where I earned a BSc in Wildlife Science and Conservation. Those years were transformative — training at Tarangire National Park, conducting bird surveys, large mammal counts, and community conservation work.

Since February 2024, I have been working as a tour guide at Mikumi National Park, conducting game drives and managing the full safari experience for visitors from around the world. Ghikas Adventures grew out of everything I had learned — and everything I believed was missing in the market.

"Tanzania does not need to be sold. It only needs to be seen."
